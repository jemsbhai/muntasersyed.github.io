About / Muntaser Syed

About my work.

I write papers and build software about trust, efficiency, and intelligence in computing systems.

Research focus

AI systems should report their limits.

My current work covers confidence representation, provenance, temporal validity, context budgets, and edge deployment.

My doctoral research in computer engineering at Florida Institute of Technology focused on artificial intelligence of things and effective machine learning on edge devices. Related projects cover semantic data, agent infrastructure, energy measurement, health sensing, compact data structures, and explainability.

I also teach through workshops and public repositories. Prototypes help me evaluate the methods in practice.
